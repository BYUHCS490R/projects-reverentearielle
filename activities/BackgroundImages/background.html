Engagement - Background Images

    When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag?
  1. You should use a background image when the image is part of the design or decoration, like a background texture or banner that does not contain important content.
  2. You should use the tag when the image is part of the content of the page, like a photo, logo, or anything important that users need to see and understand.
