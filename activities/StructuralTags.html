Engagement - Structural Tags

  1. What goes in the header?
    The header contains introductory content such as the site title,logo, headings and sometimes navigation
  2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?
    The Nav contains navigation links. It can go inside the header but it does not have to. The nav usually links to pages withinthe same site, but it can also link to other website
  3. What goes in Main?
    The main tag contains the primary content of the page, such as text, images, and articles that are unique to that page.
  4. What goes in the Footer?
    The footer contains information such as copyright details contact information, author info, or legal links.
