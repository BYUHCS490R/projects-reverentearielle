Payment Method.

Customers can pay using cash or card. That makes the booth easy to use for different people. Some students might have cash, while others may prefer using a card.

I want the payment system to be fast and simple so people do not have to wait long. The machine should show the payment options clearly on the screen.

Since this booth is near a high school, the payment method should be easy enough for students to understand right away. It should feel quick, safe, and convenient.

Digital payment systems are also very popular today. For example,Google Pay and Apple Pay make it easy for users to pay quickly using their phone or card without needing cash.
