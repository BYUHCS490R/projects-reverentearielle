Features

The photobooth allows users to choose different templates before taking photos. This gives people options so their pictures can look fun, cute, or more classic.

Each session includes four pictures in one layout. After taking photos, the booth prints two copies automatically. The camera and screen guide the user step by step so the process feels easy.

I also want the booth to have a bright screen, simple instructions, and a clean design. That way, students can use it quickly without getting confused.
