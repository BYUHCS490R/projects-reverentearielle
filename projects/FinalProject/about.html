About the Photobooth

This photobooth is made for anyone who wants quick, fun, and aesthetic photos. It’s fully automated, so you can just walk in, use it on your own, and enjoy the experience without needing help.

All you have to do is step inside, follow the instructions on the screen, and start snapping pictures. The process is simple, fast, and easy, which makes it perfect for busy places like schools or events.

I chose this idea because taking photos is something people enjoy, especially with friends. It’s a fun way to capture memories, and I think this kind of business is creative, practical, and something people would actually use.
