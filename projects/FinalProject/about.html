About the Photobooth

This photobooth business is designed for students who want fun and quick photos. It is fully automated, so users can use it without any staff.

Customers can step inside the booth, follow instructions on the screen, and take pictures. The system is simple and easy to use.

This business is perfect for schools because students enjoy taking photos with friends.
