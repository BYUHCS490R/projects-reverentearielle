About the Photobooth

This photobooth business is designed for students who want fun and quick photos. It is fully automated, so users can use it without any staff.

Customers can step inside the booth, follow instructions on the screen, and take pictures. The system is simple and easy to use, which makes it a good choice for a busy school area.

I picked this idea because photobooths are fun, and students usually like taking pictures with friends. I also think it would be a nice business because it is small, useful, and easy to understand.
