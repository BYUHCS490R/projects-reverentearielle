Title and Topic

Title: My Dream Photobooth Business

For my final project, I want to create a website about my dream business, which is an automated photobooth near a high school. Students can take pictures, choose a template on the screen, and pay using cash or card. After that, the machine will print two copies automatically. I chose this topic because it is something I am interested in and I think it would be a fun and useful business idea.

Content Pages

  • Home Page: A simple introduction to my photobooth business.
  • Page 1: How the photobooth works step by step.
  • Page 2: Different templates and photo styles users can choose.
  • Page 3: Pricing and payment options (cash or card).
  • Page 4: Why placing the booth near a high school is a good idea.
  • Page 5 (Table Page): A table showing packages, prices, and number of photo prints.

Form and Table

The form will ask for name, email, number of copies, and preferred photo template. Users can also leave a comment or suggestion. The table will show different photobooth packages, prices, and what each package includes.

Content and References

Most of the content will come from my own ideas and what I imagine for my business. For images, I will use free websites and make sure they are allowed to use. I will also look at other photobooth businesses for ideas, but I will write everything in my own words.
