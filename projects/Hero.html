Hero: Jennie Kim (BLACKPINK)

Jennie Kim is a South Korean singer and rapper who is a member of the K-pop group BLACKPINK. I admire Jennie because of her confidence, stage presence, and versatility as an artist. She is known for being both powerful and elegant, and she inspires many fans through her music, fashion, and individuality.
